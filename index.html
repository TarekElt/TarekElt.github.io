Who are you Tman?

Whats up! My name is Tarek Eltantawy (Tman) and im a creator and artist. Im currently a student at the University Of Miami studying Computer Science and minoring in art.

I love to use my hands and have been ever since I was young. It started with me upcycling and repurposing old junk around the house and turning them into toys to play with. I never considered myself an artist at first, I was just doing what I enjoyed, what felt natural. I work in many mediums but my most prevalent are wood and ink. My creations are all handmade and can vary in design or functionality. They could also be purely decorative. I'm extremely grateful for my creative ability and all the opportunities and joy it has given me.

Im currently working towards my goal of creating a brand with the goal of "selling" a version of life full of creative living. in terms of actaully selling things, i will be selling many creations from many differnt mediums including sculptures, clothes, and prints.

Let me not delay any further. Welcome to my website!
